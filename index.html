FAQ

What separates StoryX from other top UX and web design agencies?
We’re a web design agency that believes a great website should efficiently convert visitors to customers while providing a delightful web experience that’s true to the brand. Good UX UI design of a digital product or website leads to higher conversion rates. That’s why unlike other web design agencies, we have UX designers collaborate with web designers on all website design projects.
How much does it cost to hire StoryX for project?
Our pricing is directly related to the project scope, timeline, deliverables, and team composition. Most of our web design project are fixed‑price contracts. In this case, we’ll give you a detailed proposal after learning as much as possible about your project. All estimates are calculated based on the hourly rates and the number of hours we think the job will take.
What process should I expect from your UX agency?
Our process is based on the design thinking methodology, which allows us to be flexible, adapting to almost any project type and collaboration model. While each project is unique, we always go through the following key phases:
Story
A great website needs great content, we use our unique storytelling process to help our clients tell meaningful stories that create an emotional connection with their future customers. We’ll help define your digital product strategy.
Design
UX and UI design is the balancing act between beauty and functionality. By understanding the value you want to deliver for your customers we create digital products that people love to use and achieve your business goals.
Development
We love taking on ambitious projects and always look for ways to use technology to create impactful solutions. We code all of our websites using a variety of languages enabling endless creativity.
Support
You won’t need to worry about your website with our maintenance and support plans. These are designed to continuously add value and support you in getting the most from your web presence.
How can I get a proposal for my project?
If you're looking for a design agency, please send us an email first with a quick summary of your project. During our first call, we will ask a lot of questions about the project goals, audience, budget, timeline, and other essential details. We will email you a proposal and can schedule a video call to walk you through it.
