Stunning Websites

A website is an essential part of any successful business. You know what it feels like to stumble across a website that pushes the boundaries of what is known to be “pleasing” on the internet. These are the websites that make you stop and think, scroll and click and get you thinking "How could this business help us?"

That Tell Compelling Stories

Businesses are faced with endless chances to tell a better story. Most websites hand out a bunch of statements of fact, and facts don't convince anyone of anything. Stories do.

By taking users on a journey and allowing them to experience your brand through narratives, you forge a stronger connection and leave a lasting impression. Stories transcend mere facts and have the ability to inspire, persuade, and build long-term relationships with your audience.

3

3 seconds is the time in which you want to make sure that your website is going to load. That is the maximum time you should expect somebody to wait to load your website!

5

The main rule in web design is all about speed: If a visitor doesn’t figure out your content, your product or what your website is about in 5 seconds, you’ve probably lost them.

30

Mass marketing will not engage the new consumer, whose selective hearing is well developed. Instead, marketers have 30 seconds in which to appeal directly to an individual.
