Our Commitments

We believe in technology being a force for good so we launched Accelerate Zero to build affordable technology for organisations tackling the toughest problems facing people and the planet.

We're a regenerative business
We have a social and environmental purpose at the core of our businesses. We have been set up to support ethical, sustainable, local businesses and organisations by building affordable, world class websites. We go beyond current notions of sustainability, using our voice to promote shifting our economy away from its roots of extraction and exploitation towards caretaking and regeneration.
Reuse, reduce, recycle
The choices we make, collectively can change the world. We actively try to reduce the amount of waste we produce and use refurbished technology where possible.
We declare a climate emergency
We have declared a Climate Emergency and prioritise business decisions that are good for both people and planet.
Partnerships for the goals
If we’re to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals by 2030, every business needs to understand how they can contribute to them. We have aligned our business to support the goals and we help the businesses we work with to do the same.
